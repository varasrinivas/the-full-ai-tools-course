T-07 / M-7.3 · The Context Window Is a Budget DUAL-PATH LAB
Concept · 12 min

Your agent packs a backpack, not a warehouse

Real-world analogy An astronaut on a spacewalk carries only what the mission needs. Every extra kilogram costs fuel. Your agent's context window works the same way — every stale file, every old stack trace you leave in the session is mass it hauls on every single turn.

Watch the window fill as a session runs: system prompt, then your CLAUDE.md memory, then code, then conversation — then the junk. When the debris crosses the line, /compact fires and squeezes the session back to what matters.
